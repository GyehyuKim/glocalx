## Article 1 — Contract Period & Terms

① This contract is a short-term lease based on single occupancy (one person per room), calculated in 30-day units. Rent must be paid in advance.

② If the tenant does not notify the management of their intention to vacate at least **15 days before** the end of the contract period, the contract is automatically renewed under the same terms.

③ The minimum stay is one (1) month. If the tenant vacates before completing one month, no refund will be issued.

④ For move-out after the first month, the tenant must notify management in writing (text message, KakaoTalk, etc.) at least **15 days prior** to the intended move-out date.

① Monthly rent: \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ KRW (utilities and management fees included)

② Rent is due on the same date each month, based on the original move-in date.

③ **Refund Policy — Short-Term Daily Rate Applied**

In the event of early move-out, refunds are calculated using the **short-term daily rate** (not a simple pro-rated daily calculation).

- Short-term daily rate: \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ KRW / day
- Refund amount = Rent already paid − (Short-term daily rate × Actual days stayed)
- If the calculation results in a negative amount, the difference will be deducted from the security deposit.

---

## Article 3 — Security Deposit Refund

① The security deposit will be refunded within **3 business days** after move-out, after deducting any applicable fees including cleaning fees and costs for damages or contamination.

② A basic cleaning fee of **30,000 KRW** will be deducted from the security deposit.

③ Additional charges for damages or contamination beyond normal wear and tear will be billed at actual cost.

④ **For foreign tenants (international wire transfer)**: If the security deposit is refunded via international wire transfer, all wire transfer fees and related charges are the responsibility of the tenant. The refund amount will be the net amount after deducting such fees.

---

## Article 4 — Tenant Obligations

① The tenant may not sublet or transfer the room to any other person.

② Smoking, violence, excessive noise, and any illegal activities are strictly prohibited within the facility.

③ The tenant is solely responsible for the management of personal belongings and valuables. The management is not liable for any loss or theft.

④ Common areas (kitchen, bathroom, laundry room, etc.) must be kept clean and used with consideration for other tenants.

⑤ The tenant must comply with all facility rules and regulations.

---

## Article 5 — Termination & Eviction

① The management reserves the right to immediately terminate this contract and require the tenant to vacate under any of the following circumstances:

1. Failure to pay rent for 7 or more days past the due date
2. Repeated violation of facility rules
3. Causing serious harm to other tenants or the facility

② Any costs incurred due to forced eviction (e.g., moving costs, restoration costs) will be deducted from the security deposit.

---

## Article 6 — Visitor & Access Policy

① Visitors are not permitted inside the facility as a general rule.

② In unavoidable circumstances, visitors may be allowed with the prior approval of the management, strictly within the minimum necessary scope.

③ The facility management may enter the tenant's room when necessary for safety or maintenance purposes. In cases of emergency, entry may be made **without prior consent**.

---

## Article 7 — Abandoned Belongings

① All personal belongings must be removed by the tenant at the time of move-out.

② Any items left behind after move-out will be stored for 5 days. Any costs incurred during the storage and disposal process are the responsibility of the tenant. Items not claimed within 5 days will be disposed of at the management's discretion.

③ Disposal costs may be deducted from the security deposit.

---

## Article 8 — CCTV

① CCTV cameras operate in common areas of the facility (hallways, entrances, kitchen, etc.) for the safety and security of all residents.

② By signing this contract, the tenant acknowledges and consents to the installation and operation of CCTV cameras in common areas.

---

## Article 9 — General Provisions

① Matters not specified in this contract shall be governed by applicable laws and facility regulations.

② In the event of a dispute, both parties shall first attempt to resolve the matter through mutual discussion. If unresolved, either party may submit the matter to the competent court.
